- More About Me
An ordinary creative with
extraordinary vision
It all began when I started college. I explored my creative side during my early college days while doing projects, photo editing & making tattoos for friends & mates. Later, I picked up my interest in Hyper-Realistic Sketching.

Eventually, I convinced myself with my works & praise I got from my surrounding ones and started designing with the small stuffs, be it an illustration or even smaller icons. Then, I was introduced to the very term Ui/Ux Design via one YouTube video from ABNUX by Abhinav Chhikara.

I began learning as much as I could for almost a year while studying my under graduation in computer engineering. Still learning. After doing freelancing and on-site work for 2.5 years, here I am with a handful of valuable experiences & projects, Still hoping to get more : )
